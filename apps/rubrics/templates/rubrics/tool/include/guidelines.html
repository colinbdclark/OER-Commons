• Before assessing the alignment of OERs to standards, users should have access to the Common Core State Standards (CCSS). These can be found at www.corestandards.org the-standards.
  • There are five possible ratings within each rubric: Superior, Strong, Limited, Very Weak, or N/A.
  • Each rubric should be scored independently of the others.
  • Users can apply up to 7 rubrics to an OER.
  • Mark “N/A” on any rubric that doesn’t apply to the resource you are evaluating.
  • Most of the rubrics can be applied across content areas and object types, but Rubric VII currently applies to mathematics.
  • A user’s review of an object is complete once the ratings are submitted through the “Finalize OER Review” button at the bottom of the “Opportunities for Deeper Learning” rubric.
